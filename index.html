About me
About me

I have a wealth of experience in this sector

I am a professor of English and an educationist. I have held leadership positions at Anambra State University and is an international examiner in education. She is also an author and an advocate for education and women's rights. Here is more about me:

Education
  • Holds a double Masters Degrees, (Med) Educational Administration, 1997) and (MA English 2004).
  • Holds two Doctorate Degrees, PhD Educational Administration (2002), PhD English { Comparative Literature stress } (2010).
Career
  • Ngozi serves as the Dean of Arts and University Orator at Chukwuemeka Odumegwu Ojukwu University.
  • Ngozi has received National and State Awards as an outstanding Literary Artist.
  • Prof. has also Authored 30 books and Published 95 articles in academic journals and is an advocate for the power of arts and literature to address global issues.
Other
  • Ngozi is affiliated with several academic and literary organizations.
  • She Focuses on eliminating illiteracy among Nigerian women, Researches on women's development and protection, especially in conflict areas.
  • She has spoken at 65 conferences, delivered 12 keynotes, and given lectures across Nigeria and international universities.
Vision and Beliefs

I am guided by a set of core values and beliefs that inform my decisions and actions, such as:

  • Equality, justice and compassion
  • Accountability and fiscal responsibility
  • Integrity, honesty and trustworthiness
  • Innovation, progress and sustainability
