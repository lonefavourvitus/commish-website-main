Hon. Pete Ibida Emerges As New Majority Leader Anambra State Assembly

The Anambra State House of Assembly has appointed with immediate effect, Dr Pete Ibida, representing Njikoka ll constituency as its new Chief Whip. Ibida’s appointment followed the message from the leadership of the All Progressives Grand Alliance, which was read by the Speaker, Mr Uche Okafor on Thursday, at the plenary. Okafor said members of APGA which is the majority party in the House met and appointed Dr Pete Ibida representing Njikoka ll constituency, as the new Chief Whip of the House. Responding, Ibida appreciated the House for finding him worthy to serve as the new Chief Whip. “I promise to use this position to champion excellence for the interest and progress of the Anambra state Assembly,” Ibida said. The House Majority Leader, Dr. Nnamdi Okafor, extended congratulations to Ibida on behalf of the lawmakers, assuring him of their support. It could be recalled that on February 16, Mr. Lawrence Ezeudu, the former Chief Whip representing Dunukofia Constituency, resigned from his position with immediate effect, as conveyed in a letter dated December 7, 2021.

Hon Dr Pete Ibida for Dunukofia, Njikoka and Anaocha Federal Constituency

A fifty-five-man campaign organization for Pete Ibida was inaugurated with a charge to actualize the federal House of Representatives ambition of the lawmaker. The inauguration, held at the country home of the lawmaker in Nimo, Njikoka Local Government Area of Anambra State, appointed Mr. Patrick Onyedum as the Director General of the campaign organization. Hon. Ibida, a two-time member of the Anambra State House of Assembly, recounted the numerous projects he had undertaken in each ward in his constituency. He promised to double his performance as a federal lawmaker during the 2023 general election. Describing himself as an experienced facilitator and advocate of good governance, Honourable Ibida asserted that he is not going to the National Assembly as a novice but as a lawmaker who knows how to exploit opportunities to attract democracy dividends for his constituency. In inaugurating the members of the campaign organization, the lawmaker characterized them as high performers and a winning team committed to doing all that is necessary to achieve their goals.

The Making of Akalaugo n' Awka - Hon Sir Pete Peter Ibida

On December 30, 2021, during the annual festival of Egwu Uzu in the Ancient Kingdom of Awka, His Royal Majesty, Obi Dr. Gibson Nwabueze Nwosu (Eze Uzu II), conferred the prestigious Chieftaincy title of Akalaugo Awka upon Hon. Sir Dr. Pete Ibida, Member Representing Njikoka II Constituency. Eze Uzu II hailed Hon. Ibida as an outstanding legislator, employer of labor, and philanthropist who has utilized his wealth, contacts, and knowledge to enhance society. He commended Ibida's significant contributions to the development of Awka through investments and job creation, hence the conferment of the title AKALAUGO. Addressing the media, Hon. Pete Ibida expressed his gratitude, stating, "It is a great honor for me as Eze Uzu II and the entire ndi Awka to find me worthy of this title. Truly, I am motivated to do more for the good of mankind." Hon. Ibida attended the event with his wife, Iyom Nneka Ibida, children, elder brother, and wife Ichie Sir and Lady Tony Ibida (Agunaecheibe), along with friends and well-wishers. Congratulations to Akalaugo!!!
